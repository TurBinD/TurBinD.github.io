Welcome to TurBinD

 

TurBinD is a student run venture for the University of Maryland Do Good Challenge, an annual competition for socially and environmentally impactful projects and ventures. Our team is dedicated to raising awareness for sustainable energy on campus. We have developed a small, eye catching, turbine to be used as a charging station for students. We hope this increases the sustainable face of campus and influences students and the community to learn more about wind energy and how the campus is already using wind power.
